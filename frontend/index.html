AI engine

Score fusion

No single test gives the full picture. The fusion model combines outputs from all four models into one weighted diagnosis.

⚖️
Weighted aggregation
Each model outputs a confidence score. Fusion weights are calibrated against clinical benchmarks.
🔗
Cross-modal correlation
Patterns that co-occur across tests are surfaced — signals invisible when each test is read in isolation.
📊
Confidence intervals
Borderline results are flagged clearly, signalling when clinical follow-up is warranted.
🩺
Condition-specific scores
Separate risk scores for Dysgraphia, Dysarthria, and Dyslexia, plus an overall Learning Disability Risk Index.
